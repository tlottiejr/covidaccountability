Why Report & Why Now

Your report could prevent another person from sustaining a serious adverse event, including death. After years of litigation and investigation, accountability and restitution have been limited. Filing a complaint provides another pathway toward accountability, and the statute of limitations is often longer (or nonexistent) in some states.

The vaccination campaign began in December 2020 and continued while reports of serious adverse events accumulated. Many analyses, reports and debates have followed. Your report now can help advance accountability.
