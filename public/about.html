About Us

We help injured parties navigate official reporting while preserving their voice. Our tools connect you to the correct state boards and let you prepare your account locally — we do not store your report content.

Mission

Enable timely, accurate reporting to state boards through clear guidance and practical, privacy-first tools.

What We Do

  • Maintain accurate links for every state’s official complaint portals (including multiple boards where applicable).
  • Provide a local Information Template and a Complaint Portal to streamline filing.
  • Curate references and, when appropriate, surface video materials to support reporting.

Core Values

  • Integrity — say what we do, do what we say.
  • Privacy — keep user reports on the user’s device.
  • Clarity — plain language, straightforward paths.

Next Steps

Start with the Information Template, then file through the Complaint Portal. To explore background materials, visit our References.
