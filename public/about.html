About Us

We help injured parties navigate official reporting while preserving their voice. Our approach is simple: provide clear guidance, connect people to the correct boards, and never store their report content.

Mission · Goals · Core Values

Mission

Enable timely, accurate reporting to state boards by providing a clear path and practical tools.

Goals

  • Point people to the correct official complaint portals for every state and applicable boards.
  • Help users prepare their account locally (no server storage).
  • Maintain link accuracy and uptime through audits and manual verification.

Core Values

  • Integrity — do what we say, and say what we do.
  • Privacy — keep user reports client-side.
  • Clarity — plain language, straightforward paths.
