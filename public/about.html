About

ABOUT US

COVIDAccountabilityNow is a community which understands that the COVID modRNA gene treatment resulted in unprecedented harm to the US population as documented in CDC VAERS and V-safe reporting systems as well as numerous other sources. https://openvaers.com/

WHAT WE OFFER

Free assistance with filing your injury to the medical board and support for advancing accountability.

MISSION

COVIDAccountabilityNow is committed to assist those suffering adverse events with reporting their injury to the appropriate medical board for further investigation and accountability.

GOALS

  1. Facilitate wider public attention to and transparency for problems associated with the gene based treatment.
  2. Expand funding for nonprofit organizations attempting to assist those in need.
  3. Decelerate the ongoing morbidity and mortality associated with this treatment.
  4. Foster physician reeducation involving basic interpretation of the medical literature and medical ethics for the general benefit of public health.
  5. Curtail misinformation spread by public health officials. Congressional report
  6. Prevent future “mass iatrogenic” events. Southern Hemisphere study

CORE VALUES

  1. Critical thinking and proper scientific methodology in vaccine clinical research.
  2. Physician competency at benefit-risk assessment regarding new medical treatments.
  3. Physician competency at communicating risks and benefits to patients.
  4. Ethical behavior in medicine, including respect for patient autonomy and informed consent or refusal.
