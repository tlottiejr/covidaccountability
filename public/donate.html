Support the Mission

Any funds received will be used to support the infrastructure of the site, and support injured parties involved in filing a complaint.

We operate at the highest level of integrity. Donations are used to maintain and improve the infrastructure that makes this mission possible (hosting, bandwidth, security, accessibility, and ongoing development).

We intend to tithe a portion of proceeds and, as resources allow, provide direct support to injured parties preparing and filing complaints. As donations scale (e.g., five figures), we also plan to make a dedicated contribution to the REACT19 founder with a message of contact.

Thank you for helping us keep this effort sustainable and independent.
