Who can report & who can be reported

Who Can Report And Who Can Be Reported

Anyone who has experienced a significant or serious adverse event following a COVID modRNA injection administered under the direction or advice of a U.S. licensed physician and is within the statute of limitations (varying by state) can file a report. You may also file for a loved one who has been incapacitated or has died following the treatment.

Physicians who may be reported

  • Private practice community physicians
  • Employed health system physicians
  • Chief medical officers (CMOs) of health systems where the injection was given
  • CMOs of companies (e.g., pharmacies) where the injection was administered
  • Medical directors of public health departments where the injection was received
  • Public health officials who recommended the injection

Reference: FDA — What is a serious adverse event?
